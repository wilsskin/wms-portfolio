Design Process

AI tools give me superpowers.

Claude has transformed what I can accomplish alone. Alongside the productivity gains, I can quickly build a functional prototype, share it with users, and gather feedback to validate the direction.

Figma is still where I do my design thinking. AI is great at distilling research and building prototypes, but it can't replace the human empathy needed to refine information architecture and the small details that define a product.
